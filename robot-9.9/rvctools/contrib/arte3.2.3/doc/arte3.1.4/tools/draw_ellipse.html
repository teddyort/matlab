DESCRIPTION 

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
   DRAW_ELLIPSE(POS, COV, COLOR) 
   Draws an ellipse in the current figure with color COLOR, 'r', 'g', 'b'...etc 
   The ellipse is drawn at position POS and defined by its covariance
   matrix COV.
   
    See also DRAW_CIRCLE, DRAWROBOT3D.
